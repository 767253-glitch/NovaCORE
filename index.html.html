Why Oak Ridge, Tennessee?
01
Nuclear workforce — Oak Ridge National Laboratory & TVA have trained a deep nuclear talent pool for 70+ years.
02
TVA partnership — Tennessee Valley Authority is the most nuclear-experienced utility in the US; an ideal grid off-take partner.
03
Cooling water — The Tennessee River provides the reliable cooling water supply SMRs require.
04
Low land & power costs — Industrial land ~$5K/acre vs $200K+ in Northern Virginia. TVA rates are historically the lowest in the US.
05
Fiber & connectivity — Major internet exchange points in Atlanta and Charlotte are within 250 miles, keeping latency competitive.

Full-stack is the right call for NovaCORE

Selling power wholesale to the grid at $65/MWh earns ~$57M/year. Selling that same power as AI compute earns ~$432M/year — a 7.6× revenue multiplier on the same electrons. Co-locating the reactor with the data center also eliminates transmission losses, grid congestion risk, and the #1 cost for any competing data center operator. The carbon-free story lets NovaCORE charge a 15–25% premium over fossil-grid competitors.
